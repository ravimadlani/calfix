Scheduling Enhancements

Exploration of how the scheduling modal and workflow can evolve to respect multi-timezone working hours, remove provider-specific language, and improve the flow from gathering participant details to sending suggested meeting windows.

Each section below represents a distinct UX flow with the core interactions highlighted. Use this document as a conversation starter before investing in high-fidelity designs.
